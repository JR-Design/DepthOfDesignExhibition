Depth of Design

Welcome to Depth of Design!

The Cambrian College Grahic Design class of 2021 are proud to share with you a digital exhibit showcasing their design work from the past three years. Depth of Design aims to display the techniques used for a project and the concept behind it, coming together to create a successful and impactful final deliverable.

We invite you to browse the diverse projects that range from branding to web design and everything in between, designed by our grads of 2021.

Meet the Students

Depth of Design is the exhibit created by students for the students. Take a look at the graduating class who brought this concept to reality!
