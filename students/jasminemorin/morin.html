Still Good

Where wastage meets Scandanavian.


Still good is a brand I have come up with due to the major issue of food waste in grocery stores. Whether it is expired food, overstock or damaged goods, over 43 billion tons of food gets wasted each year. I have come up with a solution by creating an app that targets bread and bakery products. This app is for Ontarians to purchase and pick up close to expiry or damaged bread and bakery items locally at discounted rates. The goal is to reduce such high amount of bakery wastage as 25% of bread that is on the shelf gets wasted. The app is designed to serve the users needs. It is simple and friendly, as well as sophisticated.

The user is able to give their location and find products at a reduced price at grocery stores nearby. This is more useful for those who don’t have a vehicle and walk to the store. As well, college students, young adults and young parents. They are able to create a profile and add the information they choose to. The user has a search option to find the store they want to shop at and the products they want to purchase. The app has a rewards system to promote more purchasing and receiving offers based on what the consumer buys.

Skateboard Merch

Where abnormal meets trendy.


For this piece I had full creative design. I decided to go out of my comfort zone and create a piece that has some exploration to it. Adding limbs and eyeballs in a fun way and not gruesome works well with a skate park theme, falling and getting back up. The center piece is to push the background pattern further and have a main visual. It’s bright, youthful and appealing to the eye.

Pure Leaf Package

Where packaging meets minimalism.


This package design is perfect for a warm summer day with friends and family. This aims for the demographic of young families who enjoy summer activities. It is geometric and clean line work that is modern. This design would be year round to keep the excitement of that summer feeling in a bottle. The bottle design is a piece of the full design that still gives out the warm summer day message.
