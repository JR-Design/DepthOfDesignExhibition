Stamps Design

Where promotion meets illustration


Creating stamps for the International Year of Fruits and Vegetables by promoting healthy diets and keeping your heart happy by eating a little healthier. The use of bright colours and heart shaped fruit and vegetable illustrations creates a more fun and inviting concept, leaving the consumer intrigued.

Event Launch
In addition to creating stamps, an event was created to not only promote the stamps, but to promote the International Year of Fruits and Vegetables as well.

Amazon Echo Dot Manual

Where photography meets layout


Amazon product manual for the Echo Dot to showcase the different features. The goal was to keep the same general style and colour palette, but incorporate new product photography. Ensuring that every aspect of the product is shown, and is presented with a polished look.

Liberty Travel Promotion

Where promotion meets simplicity


This advertisement campaign was to showcase a travel destination to promote their package deals. Using illustrations to support the destination of Petra, Jordan. The simple design leaves out details, leading consumers to the website to learn more and see more of the travel location.
