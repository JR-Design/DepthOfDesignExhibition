PCOS care

Where knowledge meets femininity.


PCOS care designed to make people aware of this unfamiliar disease PCOS (Polycystic ovary syndrome). The target audience for the campaign is the age group between 19-35 years.

Project Brief:

PCOS is the health condition of women with hormonal imbalance. Sometimes, women are well-informed about their health. It causes other diseases such as diabetes, heart disease, obesity, anxiety and uterine cancer. PCOS care aware people regarding knowledge of the PCOS which, leads to a better cure.
