A silent Voice Movie Poster

Where film meets minimalism


This project is for a movie poster that is for a movie called the silent voice, I decided to go for a minimalist approach with it, to mostly keep people wondering what the movie would be about, so they would go watch it, I decided to have an ear with a hearing aid on it, to show the fact that the main character is deaf, I have it set so the character is falling from it, to mostly get people wondering.

ScrabbleLatte Advertisement

Where entertainment meets advertisement


This project is for the company called Scrabblelatte, the goal for this was to create something that would get people to come in and try their themed drinks, I decided to focus on the drinks that had to do with more popular things, such as pokemon and Harry Potter.
