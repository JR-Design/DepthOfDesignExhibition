Own Grown

Where waste meets awareness


International Year of Fruits and Vegetables stamps were a concept for Canada Post. The campaign would not only have the stamps and limited edition packaging but would also hold an event to bring attention to food waste across the world. So much food is thrown away and considered bad when in reality fruits and vegetables can be replanted from just some scraps. Growing from home has been more popular than ever due to the pandemic so bringing more attention to growing methods and the amount of waste created each year would be the ultimate goal of this event.

Bamboo Razor

Where sustainability meets production


Bamboo Razor was a concept product to create a more sustainable eco-friendly razor and attempt to move on from non-recyclable disposable razors. This campaign consisted of 3 poster ads, 1 billboard, and packaging for both the shipping and product boxes. The shipping box can also be ripped from the perforated edges to become an instore stand as well.

East Asian Cuisine

Where culture meets publishing


East Asian Cuisine is a short mock recipe book to display some of my food photography. This book is comprised of 9 photos and 5 recipes laid out in a linear format to allow a full page spread for both the photos and the recipe. Due to the spread of Covid, these photos were taken in an in-home studio. Each page has markers to discern the type of dish each recipe is for, main, appetizer, or sushi.
