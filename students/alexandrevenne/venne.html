Twiggs Rebrand

Where falvour and ambiance meets abstraction


With this project, I was tasked with rebranding a local or national brand. I chose the former and rebranding the Twiggs retaurant off the Kingsway. It was a fun and interesting project to tackle as I had the challenge of taking the current brand identity and rework it into one that would lend itself at least in some ways better to the company.

The route that I took was that of expanding on the rich and modern flavours of the food, and the welcoming ambiance of the restaurant and represent them in some way. In the end, I came up with the solution of abstracting these sentiments into red curvy shapes.
