Circuits

Where Student meets Code


A conceptualize an online community to serve under represented people and create a promotional website and identity to display my solution to this issue. I chose to create a solution for students and disadvantaged youth.

Project Brief

With technology rapidly changing the environment that kids are growing, learning and being raised in I decided to create a solution for them to stay ahead of technology. I created circuits which is coding classes for students of all ages as well as a technology fund for disadvantaged youth

I chose a gender neutral color palette to appeal to both boys and girls, since STEM fields generally have issues attracting females. I also wanted to give off a friendly, professional, approachable feeling in the work.

A Brief Overview For New Web Designers

Where Copywrighting meets Development


I took the opportunity to write a booklet about my obsession of becoming a web designer/developer. I wrote this booklet for fellow students, teachers and anyone interested in getting into the web field. It was a great reflection of my current knowledge and general advice I wish I had when I first started.

I also got way to lost in passion while writing for the topic and completely forgot it was a typography project, So I used Helvetica by accident.
