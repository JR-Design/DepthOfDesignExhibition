Bella’s Breakfast Menu

Where hearty meets photography


This menu was designed to be a take-home menu that customers can grab as they leave the restaurant and look at whenever they please. Photography was the main focus, to help showcase the various breakfast foods served at the restaurant.

GE Lightbulbs

Where duotone meets packaging


The idea for this piece was to create a package that would stand out against the competition’s white boxes. Bright colours such as the blue and pink to catch user’s attention when they look at the shelves. A special feature includes an illustration that creates the full silhouette of a lightbulb on the inside flaps.

Little Women Series

Where promotion meets minimalism


This poster was designed to showcase the four main characters of the movie Little Women without giving away too much of the story. Jo March, the main character, wishes to become a writer. Her older sister Meg wishes to become successful in life and marriage. Their younger sister Amy wishes to be a painter while the youngest sister is gifted on the piano.
